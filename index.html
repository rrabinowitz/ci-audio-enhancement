CI Auto-Tune (Vocoder-in-the-Loop)

Analyzes a segment of loaded audio, evaluates 625 enhancement combinations (including transpose mix), and selects settings that score highest after passing through the 16-channel vocoder with the active electrode map. Optimization targets CI survivability, not conventional speaker fidelity. After completion, metric cards display vocoded scores and percentage change vs baseline.

Electrode Map (MAP-Inspired)

Clinical MAP exports are not publicly available. Built-in presets derive from published CI literature. Custom maps can be imported as JSON; audiologists may complete profiles/example-custom.json with patient-specific data.

Diagnostic Vocoder

For normal-hearing developers only. Simulates CI hardware limitations via a 16-channel noise-band vocoder. Bypassed by default — enhancement chain is always active.
